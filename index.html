About Me

My name is Mia Stewart, and I am a sophomore biology major from Chicago. This is my second year at USC and I am loving it. I have three siblings and two dogs at home that I miss, but I love living where it's warm and pretty close to the ocean. I work part-time during the school year at a pet store, to afford living in South Carolina.

My favorite thing to do is hang out with my friends. We love planning family dinner nights every week, going out on weekends, and having study sessions when we all need motivating. I am currently in the process of adopting a black cat named Karma. I am so excited to be a cat mom soon!

My Future Career Plans
